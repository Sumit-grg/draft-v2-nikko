***An engagement session is highly recommended prior to the wedding. It’s a great opportunity for us to get to know each other and for you to feel comfortable with me as your photographer.***

Whats an engagement session??
An engagement session is a photo shoot typically held before the wedding day, where the couple has their photos taken in a relaxed setting. It's a chance for the couple to get comfortable in front of the camera and build a rapport with their photographer. The photos from the engagement session can be used for save-the-date cards, wedding invitations, or decor, and they also provide a way to capture the couple's personalities and relationship before the wedding day.
